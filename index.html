About

The vision of the maroon project is to code a highly modularized calendar, with interchangeable presentation modules. In order to achieve this the calendar logic and presentation are rigorously seperated. The calendar consists of small loosely coupled parts that do one thing very well while having a coherent, consistent overall design.
